# Hospital Management

## Introduction

  This is Hospital Management web application made for managing patient records on hospital.
  The objective of the hospital management app is to create a structured and efficient way to manage the 
  patient records in hospital which will help the hospitals to gain an improvement in the medical services
  and treatments they provide.

  It will also help doctors to treat patients in most effective way and make their live livable .
